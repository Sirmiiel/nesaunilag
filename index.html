WHY STUDY ECONOMICS?

INFORMED DECISION MAKING

Economics helps us understand how individuals, businesses and governments make choices.

GLOBAL PERSPECTIVE

Economics provides a lens to examine international trade, globalization and economic development.

PERSONAL FINANCE

Economics offers insights into personal finance, budgeting and investing.

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

An economics background opens the doors to diverse career paths.
