REBOUND AND RISE: REDISCOVERING RESILIENCE THROUGH SPORTS

In a world that has faced unprecedented challenges, there comes a time when the spirit of resilience needs rekindling, and that time is now. After a hiatus of three years, the school department’s much-awaited sports festival is back and better with a theme that resonates deeply with the struggles and triumphs of the recent past – “Rebound and Rise: Rediscovering Resilience through Sports.”

The memories of the last big sports festival is etched vividly in the minds of students and faculty alike mine too. I remember my first year in the school, we were welcomed into preparations for a sports festival. At the time, I did not understand it fully until the week came. I saw how dedicated and determined every student was to clinch a victory in the sport of their choosing.

The vibrant energy, the roaring cheers, and the sense of unity created an aura that lingered on long after the event had concluded.

However, the following years brought unexpected turmoil. The COVID-19 pandemic and subsequent school strikes cast a shadow over the usual enthusiasm that surrounded sports. It was a hassle trying to revive the spirit of athleticism in students after the prolonged hiatus, something I believe we weren’t able to fully achieve in the most recent sports festival.

As days turned into months, the once bustling sports fields fell silent, and the vibrant school spirit began to wane. The absence of competitive events, the lack of camaraderie, and the uncertainty of the future led to a disheartened atmosphere. But amidst this backdrop of challenges, an idea was born in my mind – an idea to reignite the flame of sportsmanship, unity, and most importantly, resilience.

And so, the concept of the “Rebound and Rise: Rediscovering resilience through sports” took shape. The theme captures the essence of what everyone has been through. It is a call to action, an invitation to dust off setbacks and rise above them, just as a basketball rebounds from the floor. The symbolism was profound – each challenge faced would become a catalyst for growth and strength.

The festival is not just about the games; it is about rebuilding connections, reigniting the competitive fire, and reminding everyone of the power of collective determination.

And so, The Theme unfolds – a day of exhilarating matches, heart-stopping moments, and a resounding reminder that the human spirit, much like a ball, can bounce back from even the hardest of impacts. The festival is more than just an event; it is a declaration that setbacks are only temporary, and the power to rise above them resided within each individual. I hope the echoes of cheers, the camaraderie, and the renewed passion for sports will linger long after the festival has concluded. The Festival will not only revive the department’s sports culture but also instill a renewed sense of resilience that will continue to guide everyone in their journey ahead.

– Victoria Ogunwemimo

The Sports Secretary Elect, Nigeria Economic Students’ Association(NESA)
